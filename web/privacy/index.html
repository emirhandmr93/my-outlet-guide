Privacy Policy

How My Outlet Guide processes information for account, trip, review, alert, weather, and currency features.

Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 12, 2026.

Information we process

  • Account email and display name.
  • Saved favorites and saved trips.
  • Flight deal alert preferences.
  • Notification settings and push token if registered.
  • Reviews, ratings, helpful votes, and review reports.
  • Moderation and account deletion records where relevant.
  • User-selected route and city data.

Device GPS

The app does not require device GPS for trip weather. Weather is based on selected trip, outlet, or city metadata when available.

Providers

My Outlet Guide uses Firebase for app backend services, Frankfurter for live currency, and Open-Meteo via backend if a weather provider is configured.

Reviews

Published reviews may be visible with your display name. After account deletion, review author identity is anonymized.

Account deletion

You can request deletion in the app or by contacting info@myoutletguide.com.

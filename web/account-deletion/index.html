In-app and external request paths for deleting a My Outlet Guide account.

Account Deletion

Last updated: July 12, 2026.

In-app path

Profile → Account management → Delete Account

External request

Contact info@myoutletguide.com from the email connected to your account when possible.

What is deleted

  • Firebase auth account.
  • Favorites.
  • Saved trips.
  • Flight deal alert preferences.
  • Notification settings and push tokens.
  • Account-linked private preferences.

What may remain or be anonymized

Published reviews may remain for outlet integrity, but author identity is anonymized. Moderation and audit records may be retained as needed for abuse prevention and integrity.

Same-email re-registration

Old anonymized reviews are not relinked to a new account if the same email is registered again.

Requests are processed after identity and account ownership checks are completed.
